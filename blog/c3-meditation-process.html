How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I had to give up my ideals of what I want to achieve with this web page....for now. I know that I'm not able to do everything that I want and from what I have seen and learnt so far, everything in coding is a learning process that you are never finished with, so focusing on the learning journey is a much better way to have satisfaction with how you are doing.


What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I have to admit that I didn't really think of meditation in terms of active meditation before. I only thought of it in terms of structured environments where people are listening to new age music and trying to achieve some level of spiritual clarity. It's nice to read about it in terms of a more scientific process and one that's focused on bettering yourself physically and behaviourally.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

I guess what I have mentioned above about the nature of meditation. Also the different ways you can use it in a simple way to centre yourself and increase your focus, which is beneficial for study and work.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

I did like the idea of active meditation and just taking a minute or so a day to try and quieten your mind. To see it like using a muscle and even when your mind wanders, that the process of bringing it back to silence and calm is increasing your level of focus.
